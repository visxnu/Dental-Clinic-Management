About Chandragiri Dental Clinic

Located in Udma, Kasaragod, Chandragiri Dental Clinic offers high-quality dental care in a welcoming and comfortable environment. Our team of experienced dentists is dedicated to providing the best dental services for you and your family.

We are open 6 days a week, providing a wide range of services, including routine check-ups, preventive care, and emergency dental treatments. Our clinic is closed on Sundays to give our team a well-deserved break.

Appointments can be easily booked by calling us directly at 07947145010. We strive to offer convenient and timely service for all our patients.

Our Working Hours

  • Monday - Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed
